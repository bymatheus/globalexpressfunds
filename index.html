How it works

1. Apply

Applicants are to respond to our questionnaire, which is available here online, for instant review.

Applicants must provide the following:

  1. Copy of a valid photo ID.
  2. Recent statement of account, endorsed by applicant's bank.
  3. Last 6 months notarized financial history, either personal or corporate.
  4. 1 page written loan application.
  5. Project summary.
  6. Spreadsheet.
  7. Income projection.
  8. Repayment schedule.
  9. Board resolution if you are applying for fund on behalf of a corporation.

2. ​Review

Our background checks and verification enables us know you better and how much cash you can access and at what terms that best meet your needs.

3. Receive Funding

Upon reaching the funding criteria, you'd be financed based on effectual review results.

Applicants are required to pay a token of US$28, this amount covers the cost of conducting Due Diligence/ Background Check on Applicants.
